Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-25

Who we are

MoodyFoody is a meal planning app developed as a personal project. The app helps you plan weekly meals, create shopping lists, and explore recipes.

What data do we collect?

  • Account information: If you sign in with Apple Sign-In, we store your Apple ID and optional display name.
  • App data: Weekly plans, shopping lists, favorites, and custom recipes are stored locally on your device and synced to the cloud if you are signed in.
  • Household data: If you create or join a household, your weekly plan, shopping list, and favorites are shared with household members.
  • Feedback: If you send feedback, the message is stored linked to your account (if signed in).

What we do NOT collect

  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT display ads

Where is your data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device via Capacitor Preferences. If you sign in, data is synced to Supabase (hosted in the EU). Data is protected with Row Level Security so that only you (and your household) can access it.

Third-party services

  • Apple Sign-In: For authentication. We receive your Apple ID and optional name.
  • TheMealDB: Open API for recipes. No personal data is sent to them.
  • Supabase: Cloud data storage and authentication.

Delete your data

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us. All local data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
